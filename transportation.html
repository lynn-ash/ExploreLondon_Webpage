Booking A Flight Made Easy

Since London is such a popular destination, tourists going to all different areas of England tend to use it as their central landing place when flying in. Because of this, London has six different airports: Heathrow, Gatwick, City Airport, Luton, Stansted, and Southend Airport.

Airlines Entering London

There are many different airlines that have flights traveling in and out of London. Each airline provides different services and different types of planes depending on the time of day. Some examples of these airlines include Delta, JetBlue, KLM, Virgin Atlantic, Air Canada, Lufthansa, and British Airways. Here, we have chose some of the more popular flight services to provide tourists with the necessary information.





Delta

Delta is one of the largest airline providers, and serves flights spread across six of the continents. Their services include Economy, Delta One, and Comfort+, which are known for their premium customer service. They are often high regarded for their dedication to comfort and safety of their customers.


Because they in the SkyTeam Alliance, Delta can connect customers to a variety of other airline services.







KLM

Recognized as one of the world's oldest airports, KLM is the Netherlands's main air travel service. Their primary location is in Amsterdam, and they service a variety of domestic and international flights that reach areas in places like Asia, Africa, North America, Europe, and the Caribbeans.


Because they in the SkyTeam Alliance, KLM can connect customers to a variety of other airline services.










Virgin Atlantic

Founded in 1984, Virgin Atlantic is known for providing customers with a unique and comfortable traveling experience. The airline provides internatioal flights to a variety of different popular locations in Canada, the United States, the Caribbean, Africa, the United Kingdom, and Asia.


Their services include Economy, Premium, and Upper Class, and they are recognized for having very comfortable cabin areas that provide customers with a fun and welcoming atmostphere.








Air Canada

Air Canada serves as Canada's largest airline service. It serves both domestic and international flights in and out of six different continents. They have over 400 airplanes, and provide different services such as economy, business, and premium economy. Air Canada is highly regarded for its exceptional customer service and its committment to maintaining sustainability efforts.


The airline is also part of the Star Alliance, meaning that it can connect their customers with a variety of other airlines that they are in partnetship with.
