What makes London such a unique city?

London is such a lively city with many historical, artistic, and cultural landmarks that help make it shine. Whether it is somewhere iconic like Big Ben, or a bustling hub like Trafalgar Square, London has so much to offer for incoming tourists. To take at London over time through a historical point of view, places like the British Musuem or the Natural History Musuem are the most ideal locations. To see and understand the history of British monarchs, the grand Westminster Abbey is the place to be.

There are also the many diverse neighborhoods that London has to offer. Areas like Covent Garden and Notting Hill are known for their quaint botiques and lively festivities such as carnivals and street performers. Looking for something a bit more upbeat and electrifying? Camden Town and Soho are the perfect places to experience nightlife, entertainment, and music.

Do you prefer something more quiet and relaxing? Well, there are many different ways to enjoy London from a more leisurely point of view. Outdoor areas such as Kew Gardens, Regent's Park, and Hyde Park provide tourists with a more relaxed way of enjoying themselves.
